For the upcoming takeoff programming contest,CPC-PC has created a banner.The banner will be hang on the top of the tower on permanent campus.Mr Jalal will do the job.

But there is a problem Mr jalal can't climb,so he needs a ladder.

You will be given the height of the tower A (meter) and distance of jalal form the tower's ground B (meter), you have to calculate the length of the ladder H in meter.
input:
input two integer values A,B denotes the height of the tower and distance from tower to jalal.
Output:
print the length of the lader H (meter) that jalal will use.

Sample:
input:
2
3
Output:
3.61
input:
3
4
Output:
5
